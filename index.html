who
we are

Garden works has been in the pest control/garden maintenance industry since 1991. Our commitment is to provide the highest degree of integrity and professionalism while protecting the health and property of our customers. Large enough to serve you but small enough to know you, Garden Works can tailor a pest control or maintenance program to suit your unique needs.

We offer a wide verity of pest control as well as garden maintenance programs for those wishing to head off problems before they arise such as a Monthly, Quarterly, Semi-Annual, and Annual service.

Through honesty and open communications, we plan to develop a bond of trust with our customers that will insure a long-term and mutually beneficial relationship. Here at Garden Works, we formulate the costs of our services to reflect the actual merits of each project and the quality of our work.

We are a Licensed Pest Control Operator from the Department of Agriculture. All pesticides used by us are approved by the Pesticide Board of Malaysia.
